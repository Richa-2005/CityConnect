CityConnect is a GovTech-focused web platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government authorities by centralizing civic issue reporting, public project tracking, urban mobility planning, and emergency alerts into a single, transparent system.

This repository contains the **Round 2 prototype** developed for the ACM-W NariYukti TechHack, with a working frontend demonstration customized for **Hubballi city**.

## ğŸš© Problem Overview

Modern Indian cities face fragmented digital systems where:
- Civic complaints are scattered across multiple portals and often go unnoticed.
- Government projects lack transparency in timelines, locations, and public impact.
- Public transport information is confusing, unreliable, or inaccessible.
- Emergency alerts during marches, riots, or disasters are delayed or inconsistent.

This fragmentation leads to **delayed responses, safety risks, wasted resources, and reduced public trust**.
